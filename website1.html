Mombasa is found in Africa Kenya County 001. It is ranked worldwide among the tourist attraction center and second largest city after Nairobi.
It offers travelers an exotic taste of the African tropics steeped in centuries of seafaring history.
Tourist's from all over flock here to enjoy the many things to do from dolphin spotting trips on traditional dhows and deep-sea fishing to diving
and snorkelling the wrecks and reefs and basking on the sun-splashed shores.
It has many pre-historic sites, game reserves and game parks. Localists also have maintained their culture.
