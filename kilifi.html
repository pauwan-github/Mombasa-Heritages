Kilifi

Kilifi is a small beach resort on the banks of Kilifi Creek where yachts bob about in the breeze. One of the top
tourist attractions here is the Mnarani Ruins, an ancient Swahili coastal settlement dating back to the 14th century.
A huge baobab tree among the ruins is rumored to be the largest on the Kenyan coast.

Kilifi is also home to top[notch golf club. At one time, Kilifi could only be reached by ferry, but in recent
years a bridge was constructed to make it more accessible.
